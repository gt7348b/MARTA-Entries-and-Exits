MARTA Entries and Exits

This site allows you to select a starting station on the MARTA system and see where the people who entered the system exited. The data is from an average weekday in October 2009 and was obtained from the information from the Atlanta Regional Commission's Transit On-Board survey. This is informational purposes only and is not affiliated with MARTA, ARC, or any official transportation agency in Atlanta. Other notes:

  • Charts are on a relative scale to themselves
  • D3js library was used to render the charts
  • Four time periods are provided - total, AM Peak (6-9 AM), PM Peak (3:30-7 PM), and off-peak (all non-peak hours)
  • The GitHub Repo is available
